    About Me

    Projects

    Altru.AI -

    Course Project

    I collaborated with assigned partners on the development of a new LLM designed to assist students and individuals with college applications and the admissions process. I also focused on the user interface and functionality of Altru AI, specifically the ‘Profile Builder,’ which allowed users to input academic and personal details to help the LLM assist with college admission questionnaires and applications. Additionally, I researched and evaluated APIs to facilitate the collection of relevant academic data and university information.

    BetterBelli -

    Course Project

    I designed and created a Prototype application focused on dietary restrictions, allowing users to navigate dining options safely, reducing health risks and providing peace of mind for those with unique dietary restrictions. This involved conducting competitive analysis to pinpoint the positives and negatives of similar applications that would help with our creation and design of BetterBelli. I utilized Figma to create low and high-fidelity prototypes of BetterBelli which were used for usability testing and overall functionality testing.
